Emergency Fund Size: Stop Guessing and Use This Simple Formula

Dec 09, 2025
By: Nisha Verma

“3–6 months” is a range, not a plan. Here’s how to pick your number.

The formula

Emergency Fund = Monthly Essentials × Risk Multiplier

Risk multipliers

  • Stable job + low dependents: 3
  • Variable income or dependents: 6
  • High volatility / single income household: 9

Where to keep it

Liquid, safe, boring. That’s the point.

FAQs
The core fund should be liquid and low-risk. Investing adds volatility right when you need stability.
