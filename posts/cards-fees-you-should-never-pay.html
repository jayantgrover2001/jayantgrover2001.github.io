Credit Card Fees You Should Never Pay (and How to Avoid Them)

Nov 20, 2025
By: Arjun Khanna

Fees are not “small.” They’re recurring taxes on forgetfulness.

The big three

  • Late payment: fix with autopay
  • Foreign transaction: pick the right card
  • Cash advance: avoid entirely
Quick fix

Autopay the statement balance. If that’s not possible, autopay the minimum to protect your history.

FAQs
No—if the benefits clearly exceed the fee for how you spend. But most people overestimate benefits and underestimate friction.
