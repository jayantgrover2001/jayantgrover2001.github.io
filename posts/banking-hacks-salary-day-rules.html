Salary Day Rules: 7 Moves to Make Before You Spend Anything

Dec 13, 2025
By: Nisha Verma

Payday is leverage. Use it before it disappears into random purchases.

Do these 7 moves

  1. Pay fixed bills.
  2. Transfer savings.
  3. Top up emergency fund.
  4. Set weekly spending caps.
  5. Clear small debts.
  6. Buy essentials in bulk.
  7. Plan one “fun” purchase—on purpose.
Note

When you plan fun, you stop guilt-spending later.

Keep it repeatable

The goal isn’t perfection. It’s a system you can repeat for 12 months.

FAQs
Do both in small amounts, but prioritize high-interest debt while keeping a basic emergency buffer.
