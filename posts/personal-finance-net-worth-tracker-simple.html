Build a Simple Net Worth Tracker (Without Becoming a Spreadsheet Person)

Nov 11, 2025
By: Arjun Khanna

Net worth tracking is useful because it creates clarity, not because it’s fancy.

The 10-minute monthly routine

  1. Record cash + bank balances.
  2. Record investments.
  3. Record debts.
  4. Compute: assets − liabilities.
netWorth = (cash + investments + assets) - (debts)

Don’t optimize too early

Consistency is the feature. Complexity is the bug.

FAQs
Monthly is enough for most people. Weekly tracking often turns into noise.
