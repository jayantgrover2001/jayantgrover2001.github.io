50/30/20 Budget: The Brutalist Version for Real Life

Dec 22, 2025
By: Riya Mehta

The 50/30/20 rule is useful, but it’s too neat. Let’s make it usable.

Step 1: define “needs” aggressively

Needs are what keep you safe and functional: rent, groceries, transport, basic utilities.

Step 2: cap lifestyle creep

“Wants” aren’t bad. But keep them honest. If a want becomes recurring, it becomes a need.

Step 3: automate the 20

Saving/investing should happen without you making the decision each month.

BucketDefaultBrutalist edit
Needs50%As low as possible without risk
Wants30%Budget “fun” in advance
Future20%Automate first, adjust later

A note on debt

If you’re paying high interest, debt payoff lives inside the “20” until it’s gone.
