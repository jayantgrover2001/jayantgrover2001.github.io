Cut Expenses Without Misery: The “One Painful Cut” Method

Nov 25, 2025
By: Nisha Verma

Most budgets fail because they’re a thousand small “no” decisions. Replace that with one decision you can feel.

How it works

  1. List your top 5 monthly categories.
  2. Pick one category to cut by 15–25% for 60 days.
  3. Automate that cut (cancel, downgrade, or cap).
One painful cut beats 30 tiny cuts that you never keep.

Example

Downgrade one subscription bundle. Put the savings into your emergency fund.

FAQs
Start with recurring subscriptions or a lifestyle expense that doesn’t impact your health or work.
