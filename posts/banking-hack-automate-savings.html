Banking Hack: The 2-Account System That Saves Money on Autopilot

Dec 25, 2025
By: Arjun Khanna

This is the simplest savings system I’ve seen actually stick. You don’t need discipline. You need separation.

The setup

  • Account A: salary in + bills out
  • Account B: savings only (no card, no UPI, no temptation)

The rule

On payday, move your savings to Account B first. Your lifestyle adjusts to what stays in Account A.

Pro move

Name Account B something aggressive, like “Do Not Touch”.

Micro-interaction mindset

Every time you see “insufficient funds” in Account A, it forces a tiny pause. That pause is the habit change.

Automation doesn’t remove freedom. It removes the need to negotiate with yourself.

Optional: weekly sweep

Set a weekly rule: anything above your buffer goes to savings.

FAQs
Start with a number you can keep doing. Even 5–10% is powerful when it’s consistent.
It can be, but a different bank can add friction—in a good way.
